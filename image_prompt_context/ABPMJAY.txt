Name: Anjali Mehra  
Age: 35  
Gender: Female  
Occupation: Textile Worker  

Anjali is a dedicated textile worker residing in a small town in India, where she juggles her work responsibilities with caring for her young son and elderly parents. As the sole breadwinner of her family, Anjali's income barely covers their basic needs, making affordable healthcare a crucial necessity. The introduction of the Ayushman Bharat - Pradhan Mantri Jan Arogya Yojana (PMJAY) has been a beacon of hope for her family. This comprehensive health coverage has alleviated her financial burdens, allowing her family access to essential medical services at a nearby modern hospital. Anjali's story reflects the transformative impact of universal health coverage, where diverse individuals like her can now face the future with relief and gratitude.
