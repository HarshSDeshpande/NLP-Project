### Character Description

**Name:** Asha Devi

**Age:** 68

**Gender:** Female

**Occupation:** Retired agricultural worker

**Background:** Asha Devi is a compassionate and resilient woman from a Scheduled Caste community in a rural Indian village. Having spent most of her life working in the fields, she now qualifies for the Antyodaya Anna Yojana scheme due to her low income and background. Asha is widowed and relies on this welfare program to fulfill her nutritional needs. As a respected elder in her community, she provides guidance and support to her grandchildren and neighbors, sharing her rich cultural heritage and wisdom. Asha's presence at the distribution point is a source of encouragement to the community she represents.
